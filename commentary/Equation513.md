This law states that left multiplication by any given element is bijective and its fourth power is the identity.

The number of magmas of size n satisfying this law grows as `exp[(3/4) log(n/e) n^2 + n^(3/2)/2 + n^(5/4) + O(n log n)]`, namely the n-th power of [OEIS sequence A001472](https://oeis.org/A001472), modulo isomorphisms.
