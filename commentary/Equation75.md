This law states that left multiplication by any given element is bijective and cubes to the identity.

The number of magmas of size n satisfying this law grows as `exp[(2/3) log(n/e) n^2 + n^(4/3) + O(n log n)]`, namely the n-th power of [OEIS sequence A001470](https://oeis.org/A001470), modulo isomorphisms.
